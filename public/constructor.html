What is Drone Racing?

Drone Racing is an exciting new competition-based sport where custom, performance quad-copter remote-controlled drones are flown through complex courses in a bid to see who's the fastest. Drones can reach 120mph as they're piloted by players wearing VR helmets to get a First-Person view from a camera mounted on each drone.
